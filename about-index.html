At st.raw we care about making sustainability accessible to all. "If everyone is moving forward together, then success takes care of itself " --Henry Ford

our drive

Studies show that minorities in the US are severely underrepresented in the environmental sustainability workforce. Meanwhile, surveys show that minorities; mostly Black and Hispanic people are gravely affected by the effects of environmental degradation. As designers who are minorities in the US we were challenged by this gap. We asked ourselves, â€œHow can we begin to drive inclusivity in the world of sustainability?

our story

Born out of the need to answer that single question, we were led to find the simplest ways to engage relevant communities. This led us to explore the world of straws. Countless studies reveal the unimaginable impact of straws on the world. However, we found that they are a major utility engrained in everyday life. Mothers planning birthdays for their children, churches organizing picnics and even college kids hosting block parties.

our promise to you

With extensive research and a deep understanding for our intended users, we have designed straws that are plush and affordable to be used for all occasions and all beverage types. Our straws will empower you in creating memories whiles saving the environment.
